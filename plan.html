Goal

The goal of the 100SMILES Dental Care website is to enhance community oral health and build long-term,trusting dentist-patient relationships by providing comprehensive,friendly and professional dental care services
Why we need this website?:
1.Improving patient knowledge and self-management of oral health through patient education and provided resources.
2.Reinforcing the clinic's image as a trusted dental provider with over 30 years of roots in the Razole community.
3.Increasing the first-time visits.
4.Increasing the appointment of Annual Dental Check-ups.

Success Evaluation

1.By gathering patient feedback on the clarity and usefulness of the website's information through embedded satisfaction surveys or follow-up communications
2.Observing the usage count of the 20% first-visit discount and the 10% annual check-up discount promoted on the site.
3.Tracking the number of first-time and annual check-up appointments made via the website form,phone and e-mail.

Target Audience

Target Audience:
Family which is including parents and child,elder people who care about their teeth,people who interested in Cosmetic Dentistry.
How to attract target audience?:
1.Truthworthy Visual Style:Using bright and clean colors combined with real photos and clinic environment images to build trust.
2.Standing out different title like "Children's Dentistry","Annual Check-up", "Cosmetic Dentistry" and "Emergency care",which can let the user find the place they want quickly.
3.Ensuring the website functions flawlessly on phones and tablets but not only on the computer.
